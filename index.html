Welcome to the Church of Pentecost
From the National Head Aps. James Raj

Apostle James Raj, the National Head of the Church of Pentecost India, is delighted to welcome everyone to this platform in the most exalted name of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. The mission of the church is to bring perishing souls to the saving knowledge of our Lord across India and beyond. The church stands for the Word of God as the ultimate authority and is built on strong biblical foundations.

Ministries
To change lives
Women's Ministry

Besides the core function of praying, the Women’s Ministry, is responsible for giving employable and vocational skills, and training in home management to the women. The Women’s Ministry organises retreats, seminars, workshops, lectures and symposia in all spheres of life, including (i) marriage enrichment, wives’ responsibilities at home, child welfare, care and education. It also carries out evangelism and counselling for the needy.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry takes care of the youth of the Church. Its student's wing, called Pentecost Students and Associates (PENSA), operates in second cycle and tertiary institutions. It is vibrant in evangelism and leadership training.The Ministry carries out evangelism, i.e., preaches, witnesses and wins souls; encourages the youth to participate fully in the programmes and activities of the Church and further exposes them to the various ministries within the Church.

Evangelism Ministry

This is the evangelistic wing of the Church. This Ministry undertakes gospel rallies, crusades, conventions, house-to-house evangelism, and other forms of evangelism, etc.These include preaching, witnessing, winning and discipling, and encouraging church members to participate in the programmes and activities of the Church and to further expose them to the various ministries within the Church.

Pentecostal Men's Ministry - PEMEM

The Pentecost Men’s Ministry (PEMEM) caters to the interest of all the men in the Church.The Men’s Ministry organises seminars, workshops, lectures and symposia on all aspects of life including: Marriage enrichment, husbands’ responsibilities at home, child care and education; business management and techniques; leadership and responsibility in the Church and the State; current affairs at both national and international levels; the teaching of basic principles of domestic law and other specialized men-related ministries.

Children's Ministry

The Children’s Ministry, meets on Sundays and trains children along biblical precepts for balanced growth. This includes lessons on Salvation, Worship and Obedience. It supports such training with games, quizzes and Sword Drills.

Church Activities
Bible Study
The Bible studies are designed in a systematic way with a curriculum every year in order for the members to grow and mature in the Word of God and it is a weekly event.
Fasting Prayers
Fasting prayers are conducted regularly on Fridays apart from prolonged fastings planned and scheduled time to time.
Discipleship
We are in the business of fulfilling the commandment of Jesus Christ to go in to all the world and make disciples of every nation. You are invited to join our discipleship programmes in your respective districts and assemblies to become an effective disciple of Jesus Christ.
Home Cells
Our Home Cells are designed to give you strong fellowship, a family bond, discussions on the 'Word' for the growth of unity and spiritual knowledge.You are welcome to join our Home Cells near you for a healthy spritual life.
